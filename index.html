Famous Food

Kolkata's Rasgulla: Iconic Bengali dessert, spongy cheese balls soaked in sugar syrup, renowned worldwide for its delicate sweetness.

Kolkata's Puchka: Beloved street snack, crispy hollow shells filled with spicy potato mixture, dunked in tangy tamarind water, irresistible.

Kolkata's Sorshe Illish: Traditional Bengali delicacy featuring Hilsa fish marinated in mustard paste, cooked to perfection, a culinary delight.

Famous Places

Kolkata's Howrah Bridge: Iconic cantilever bridge spanning the Hooghly River, connecting Kolkata and Howrah, symbolizing the city's rich heritage.

Kolkata's Dakshineswar Kali Temple: Sacred Hindu shrine dedicated to Goddess Kali, renowned for its architectural beauty and spiritual significance.

The Victoria Memorial in Kolkata is a grand marble building, commemorating Queen Victoria, blending British and Mughal architectural styles
